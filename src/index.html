Dual Bridge

Share between Kali & Windows on same PC

What is Dual Bridge?

Freeisle creates a shared exFAT disk (FREEISLE) that both Kali Linux and Windows can read and write. All your vault data — files, gallery, notes, chat — lives on this shared disk. Boot either OS and your data is right there. No USB. No internet.

🪟 On Windows: Freeisle creates Freeisle.vhd as an exFAT virtual disk (drive F/G/H).
🐉 On Kali: Freeisle creates Freeisle.img mounted at /mnt/freeisle.
🔗 Sharing: To share between OSes, use the same physical partition below — click "Scan" to find it. The disk labeled FREEISLE is your shared isle.
Click "Scan partitions" to detect your drives
